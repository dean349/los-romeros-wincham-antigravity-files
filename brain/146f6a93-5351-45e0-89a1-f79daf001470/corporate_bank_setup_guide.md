# Opening a Corporate Bank Account for Los Romeros Limited

Since time is tight and traditional high street banks (like Barclays or Lloyds) often take 2 to 6 weeks to open a business account, Philip must use a digital "challenger bank." 

These banks use your smartphone for identity verification and can usually open an account in **24 to 48 hours**. 

---

## 🏛 Top Recommendations for Los Romeros Ltd

Here are the top three choices in the UK for a clean, non-trading holding company (SPV) about to enter an MVL:

### 1. Starling Business (My #1 Recommendation)
* **What they are:** A fully licensed, top-rated digital bank in the UK. 
* **Cost:** £0 monthly fee (Free tier).
* **Why them:** They have excellent customer service, a very stable app, and their fraud/AML team is highly responsive. Since Philip will be moving a large sum of money, having a responsive team to review documentation is critical.
* **FSCS Protection:** Yes, up to £85,000.

### 2. Tide
* **What they are:** The UK's leading purely-business financial app (they are not a bank themselves, but they hold funds in ClearBank, which is a fully regulated UK clearing bank).
* **Cost:** £0 monthly fee (Tide Free tier).
* **Why them:** They are notoriously fast for setting up Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) or holding companies. They are very used to property landlords and estate agents.
* **FSCS Protection:** Yes, up to £85,000 (via ClearBank).

### 3. Monzo Business
* **What they are:** Very similar to Starling, fully licensed digital bank.
* **Cost:** £0 monthly fee (Lite tier).
* **Why them:** If Philip already uses Monzo for personal banking, opening a Monzo Business account is nearly instant because they already hold his identity verification on file.

---

> [!WARNING]
> **The Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Hurdle**
> 
> Because this is a brand-new bank account that will instantly receive a massive deposit (approx. £265,000 / €315,000) from Philip's personal account, **the bank’s automated fraud systems will almost certainly flag and temporarily freeze the transfer.** 
> 
> This is perfectly normal and legally required under UK AML laws. Philip just needs to be prepared to immediately prove where the money came from.

## 📱 Step-by-Step Instructions for Philip

### Step 1: Gather Documentation (Before applying)
Philip should have the following ready (as clear photos or PDFs on his smartphone/computer):
1. **Photo ID:** His passport or UK driving licence.
2. **Proof of Company Address:** A recent utility bill or letter from Companies House addressed to Los Romeros Limited's registered office.
3. **The Proof of Funds (Crucial):** A copy of the Spanish property sales deed (the *Escritura* translated, or at least the pages proving the sale amount and date), plus any bank transfer receipts from the Spanish Notary or Wincham showing the initial transfer of funds from Spain to his personal account.

### Step 2: Download the App and Apply
1. Download **Starling Bank** or **Tide** from the App Store on his smartphone.
2. Select **"Open a Business Account"**.
3. **Company Look-up:** The app will ask for the company name. He should type `Los Romeros Limited` or the Company Number (`06993349`). The app will pull all the data directly from Companies House.
4. **Identity Verification:** The app will ask Philip to take a photo of his ID and then record a 5-second video selfie saying a specific phrase (to prove he is a real person).

### Step 3: Answering the Business Questions
The bank will ask what the business does. It is vital Philip answers accurately so the bank understands why a non-trading company holds so much cash.
* **Industry/Sector:** "Real Estate" or "Holding Company / SPV".
* **What does the business do?** Explain: *"This is a property holding company (Special Purpose Vehicle) that just completed the sale of its sole asset, a property in Spain. The company is currently holding cash reserves pending a Members' Voluntary Liquidation (MVL)."*
* **Expected Turnover:** State £0 going forward, but note that the company holds ~£265,000 in capital from the asset sale.

### Step 4: Execute the Transfer
1. Once the account is approved (usually within 1 to 2 business days), copy the new **Sort Code** and **Account Number**.
2. Log into his *personal* UK bank account where Wincham wrongly sent the money.
3. Set up the new corporate account as a payee: `Los Romeros Limited`.
4. Initiate a test transfer of £1.00 just to ensure the link works.
5. Once the £1 arrives safely, transfer the remainder of the exact balance, using the reference: `ERROR CORRECTION - CO FUNDS`.
6. If the transfer is held or investigated, Philip simply uploads the *Escritura* and transfer receipts to the bank's customer service chat in the app to clear it.

### Step 5: Stop Touching It
Once the money is effectively ring-fenced inside the new Los Romeros corporate account, he must leave it alone entirely until the appointed Insolvency Practitioner formally takes legal control of the account for the MVL.
