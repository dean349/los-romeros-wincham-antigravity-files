### 1.3 🟠 SERIOUS IRREGULARITY #3: 3% IRNR Retention — Incorrect Characterisation

**Refund complexity:** The 3% retention of €9,450 will be held by the AEAT. Los Romeros Limited will need to file a Modelo 210 to claim back any excess (if the actual gain is less than 3% of the sale price). **If Wincham is handling this return, the same post-Brexit eligibility problem applies.**
