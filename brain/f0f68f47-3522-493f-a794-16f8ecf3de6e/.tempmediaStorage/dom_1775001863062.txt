This firm is an 'Appointed Representative' (AR). This means it can carry out certain regulated business on behalf of a 'principal firm'. A principal firm is a firm that is authorised for certain regulated activities in the UK and delegates the whole or part of its business to an AR. An AR can only carry out the regulated business that its principal firm allows it to, and the principal firm is responsible for that business.
Appointed Representatives can work on behalf of more than one principal firm.
