## Findings
- **Eligibility for Limited Companies (Ltd/LLP):**
    - Must be registered at Companies House.
    - **UK Residency Requirement:** All Persons of Significant Control (PSC) and all directors with access to the account must be individuals and **residents of the United Kingdom**. This is a major potential roadblock if the user's father is a US resident.
    - Person applying must be over 18 and a PSC/Director.
    - Standard AML, KYC, fraud, and credit checks apply.
- **Required Documents (Evidence of Trading Activity):**
    - Starling asks for documents showing what the business does and its trading activity. Examples include:
        - Signed agreements or contracts.
        - Proof of membership to an industry body.
        - Relevant qualifications.
        - Business registrations or licences.
        - **Property-related:** Solicitor documents or a decision in principle, proof of ownership or land registry details, lease agreement.
        - Bank statements showing business activity.
- **Unsupported Industries:**
    - Businesses with significant cash deposits.
    - Financial services, banking, credit.
    - Businesses holding client money (accountants, solicitors).
    - Defence, weapons, gambling, betting.
    - Unregistered charities.
    - High-value dealers (antiques, art, precious metals/stones).
