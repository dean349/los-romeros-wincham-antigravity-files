## SECTION 7 — SPANISH TAX OBLIGATIONS

### ITP — Clarification (Buyer's Tax — Not Philip's Problem)

The **ITP (Impuesto sobre Transmisiones Patrimoniales)** is the **buyer's tax** — the Spanish property transfer tax, which in the Canary Islands is **6.5% of the purchase price**. This was paid by the **buyer when they acquired** the property. It is **not Philip's/Los Romeros' obligation**.

Philip/Los Romeros' seller-side obligations are:
- **3% AEAT retention (Modelo 211)** — withheld by buyer and paid to AEAT at completion ✅ DONE
- **Modelo 210** — Los Romeros' own capital gains tax return ⚠️ OUTSTANDING — due **20 July 2026**

### Spanish CGT Calculation (Modelo 210)

```
€315,000 gross sale price
Less: Spanish estate agent commission (if seller paid)
Less: Notary + registry fees at disposal
Less: Original escritura acquisition price (2019)
Less: Purchase costs at 2019 acquisition (ITP paid by sellers, notary, registry)
Less: Capital improvements (documented renovation invoices)
= Net Taxable Gain
× 19% = Spanish CGT owed
Less: 3% retention already withheld (~€9,450)
= Refund due from AEAT (likely) OR additional payment
```

**The 3% retention is almost certainly an overpayment** — meaning a **refund of ~€9,450 (~£8,000)** should come back from AEAT. But **only if the Modelo 210 is filed by 20 July 2026**.

### Plusvalía Municipal

A separate municipal land value uplift tax payable to the Tías Ayuntamiento (Lanzarote). Following the 2021 Spanish Supreme Court reform, calculated on actual gain. **Should have been settled at completion.** Obtain a receipt from the Spanish gestoria confirming this was paid.

### Historic Annual Modelo 210 Filings (Risk Item)

Non-resident companies holding Spanish residential property (not rented) must file **annual Modelo 210 "deemed income" returns** — a small annual tax (~1.1% of cadastral value × 19%). If Wincham/Adrem did not file these for 2020–2025, there may be **arrears with interest and penalties**. Instruct the Spanish gestoria to run a compliance check directly with AEAT.
