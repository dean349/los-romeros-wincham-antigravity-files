## Design Audit Findings

### General Aesthetic
- **Forensic Legal / Tech Hybrid**: Dark, authoritative, high-contrast.
- **Theme**: "Dark Mode" by default.
- **Background**: Slate-950 (`#020617`) or similar deep slate/black.
- **Accent Colors**:
    - Gold/Yellow (`#FACC15`): Used for primary branding, logos, and key metrics.
    - Orange (`#F97316`): Used for warnings and emphasis.
    - Red (`#EF4444`): Used for high-severity alerts.
    - Blue/Sky (`#38BDF8`): Used for subheadings and informational text.
- **Typography**: 
    - Primary: High-quality sans-serif (Inter/SF Pro/Space Grotesk).
    - Features: Bold weights for emphasis, consistent letter spacing.
- **Components**:
    - **Header**: Sticky/Fixed top, minimalist logo on left, simple nav on right.
    - **Cards**: Bordered (`border-slate-800`), rounded (`rounded-xl`), often with vertical colored indicator bars on the left.
    - **Tags**: Rounded-full pills with icons.
    - **Stats Grid**: Large bold numbers with descriptive labels.

### Logo/Branding
- `⚖` (Scale of Justice) emoji used in titles and tags.
- "HARRISON EXPERT ADVISORS" logo: Serif font (likely Playfair Display) in Gold.
- "Los Romeros Limited" tags with house icon.
